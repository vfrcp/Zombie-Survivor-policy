Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the Zombie Survivor app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Maksym Zakharchuk (hereby referred to as "Service Provider", "we", "us") as an Ad Supported service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

What information does the Application collect and how is it used?

The Application uses third-party services and collects limited data for advertising, analytics, app stability, and to respond to feedback you choose to send us.

Information collected automatically (via third-party SDKs):

  • Device and advertising identifiers (including the Android Advertising ID / AD_ID)
  • A randomly generated application instance / install identifier
  • App usage and in-game event data (e.g. sessions, runs, level progress)
  • Approximate region/country (used to serve relevant ads and apply local privacy rules)
  • Crash reports and performance data

Information you choose to provide:

  • If you submit in-game feedback (a star rating and optional text), that feedback is sent to the Service Provider. Where you have consented to data collection, it may be accompanied by diagnostic context such as your install identifier, device model, operating system, and in-game statistics. If you have not consented, only the rating and text you wrote are sent.

Legal bases for processing (EEA / UK)

Where the EU/UK General Data Protection Regulation applies, we rely on:

  • Consent — for advertising and analytics. We request your consent through an in-app consent form before collecting this data, and you can withdraw it at any time (see "Your choices" below).
  • Consent / legitimate interest — for feedback you voluntarily send us, in order to read and act on it.

Does the Application collect precise location?

No. The Application does not collect precise (GPS) location. Only approximate region/country derived from your IP address may be used to serve relevant ads and apply the correct privacy rules.

Your choices and rights

Consent (EEA, UK, Switzerland): You will be asked to consent on first launch. You can change or withdraw your choice at any time via Settings → Privacy settings inside the Application.

US state privacy rights (e.g. California / CPRA): If you are a resident of a covered US state, you can opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of your personal information for personalized advertising via your device's ad settings (see below), and by contacting us using the details below.

Everyone: Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your data, to withdraw consent, and to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

You can also limit advertising on your device:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization (or Delete advertising ID)

You can stop all collection of information by the Application by uninstalling it, using the standard uninstall process for your device or app store.

Data retention

Data collected by third-party services is retained according to their respective policies. Feedback you send us is kept only as long as needed to review and respond to it. The locally generated install identifier is stored on your device until you uninstall the Application or reset it.

Children

The Application is not directed to and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly market to children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us at maksymzakharchuk8@gmail.com and we will take the necessary actions, including deleting such information.

In some jurisdictions you must be at least 16 years of age to consent to the processing of your personal information (in some countries a parent or guardian may consent on your behalf).

Security

We care about safeguarding the confidentiality of your information. The Application stores only minimal data locally on your device (such as the install identifier and your settings). Data handled by third-party services (e.g. AdMob, Firebase, Unity) is protected according to their respective security practices.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating this page and, where appropriate, through an in-app notice. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
